Conclusion

  • The teapot is quite impractical, isn't it?
  • The code is in Wingdings. Who would do that?
  • Mouse and keyboard are from Apple, but the computer is running Windows.
  • The Apple mouse is charging, but the charging port is underneath the mouse. FYI: That is the real design.
  • The coaster has a pattern that is broken at one place.
  • The mug is placed right beside the coaster instead of on top of it.
  • The notebook is has its lines drawn in the horizontal direction, but the title is written vertically.
  • The pencil has no tip. This one was for the people with eyes of an eagle.

These are all the disarrangements, we hope you liked it :)
