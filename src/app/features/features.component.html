Use in our submission:

In our scene we use mainly area lights, but we also have emissive surfaces (the monitor). To combine NEE (to best represent the lights contributions) and BSDF sampling (for the emissive surfaces), we implemented MIS.

Challenges:

It was a bit difficult to keep track of the different probabilities and weights. We had to restructure the path tracer to make it more intuitive to follow. Additionally, the introduced pdf member for the BsdfSample and DirectLightSample posed another difficulty.
